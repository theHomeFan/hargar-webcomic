A story about some kids who are friends over the internet. They decide to play a game together. There are major consequences.

The fourth, final, and most famous of the MS Paint Adventures. While it began as a "mock-game" following reader commands like the previous adventures, they were phased out as the story unfolded into a mixed-media barrage of mid-2000's internet culture satire, interpersonal drama, and Weird Plot Shit [sic].

One-upping Problem Sleuth's action-packed GIFs, Homestuck spiced up pivotal moments with Flash animations scored by a lively team of composers. Over time, the music team produced dozens of albums across a variety of genres.
